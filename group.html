I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Ohio State University, Columbus, Ohio, USA in 2022. Subsequently, I joined the Duke University and then University of California San Diego as a postdoctoral researcher.

My research is in the field of wireless and mobile computing. I work on designing and building innovative wireless communication and sensing systems for human health, safety, privacy, and security to tackle hard technical and societal problems from designing the hardware and software systems to the deployment and evaluation of these systems in real-world settings.
